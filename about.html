Our Story

Passion for food, commitment to quality, dedication to you

Welcome to One Eats

One Eats was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves access to authentic, delicious Indian food, no matter where they are. Founded in 2020, we started as a small cloud kitchen in Mumbai with a mission to bring the rich flavors of India to modern food lovers.

Today, we've grown into a trusted name in food delivery across Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities in India. Our secret? We never compromise on quality. Every dish is prepared fresh, using traditional recipes and the finest ingredients, then delivered hot to your doorstep.

We're not just a food delivery service ‚Äì we're a bridge between tradition and technology, bringing the warmth of home-cooked meals to the convenience of modern life.

Our Journey

From a small kitchen to serving thousands daily

2020 - The Beginning

Started as a small cloud kitchen in Mumbai with just 5 dishes on the menu and a dream to serve authentic Indian food.

2021 - Expansion

Expanded to 3 cities and grew our menu to 50+ dishes. Introduced our signature biryani that became an instant hit.

2023 - Recognition

Won "Best Cloud Kitchen" award and reached 10,000+ happy customers. Launched our mobile app for easier ordering.

2026 - Present

Now serving 15+ cities across India with 100+ dishes, maintaining the same quality and passion we started with.
